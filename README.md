# Walkie Talkie
<br>
<img src="https://github.com/lnx0id/WalkieTalkieItem_R.E.P.O/blob/main/mainIntro.gif?raw=true">
<br><br>
Adds a walkie talkies that you can buy at shop.<br><br>
<h2># How to use </h2>
1. You have to hold walkie talkie in hand while speaking.<br>
2. To receive sginal from another walkie talkie - your's must be out of inventory(in hand or just laying around)<br>
3. You can switch between walkie talkies (default - V) there's as much channels as walkie talkies on level.<br><br>
- Orange top number on a walkie-talkie is receiving channel<br>
and the bottom red one is the target channel(that yellow number of another walkie talkie).<br><br> <br>
